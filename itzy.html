Itzy Website

Itzy is a Girl Group based in South Korea. I redesigned their website for a project.

About the Project

ITZY is a globally renowned Korean girl group with a widespread fanbase. To cater to this international audience, I proposed the development of a dedicated fan website. This platform will serve as a central hub where fans can not only purchase albums but also access comprehensive information about the band members and their discography. The website aims to enhance fan engagement and streamline access to merchandise and band-related content.

The final deliverable is a fully functional single-page website that effectively demonstrates my expertise in front-end web development. This site features several advanced functionalities including a toggleable light/dark mode, a dynamic slideshow for displaying varied content, an interactive guessing game, and a robust contact form equipped with validation. Each element of the site has been meticulously crafted to showcase technical proficiency and enhance user engagement.
