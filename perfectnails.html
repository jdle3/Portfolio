Perfect Nails & Spa

Perfect Nails & Spa is a nail salon that is owned by my father. I wanted to create a website for him to increase his salon's online presence and increase customer retention rate.

About the Project

The existing website for the nail salon is substandard, both in terms of aesthetics and content. It features outdated information that fails to engage the salon's target audience effectively. My objective is to develop a new website that not only updates the visual and informational aspects of the salon but also enhances user experience by providing a comprehensive view of the salon's services and pricing. Additionally, the website will incorporate a gallery showcasing various nail art and designs, appealing to both new and returning customers.

The primary issue with the current website is its lackluster design and lack of regular updates, which does not reflect well on the professionalism of the salon. The site's outdated nature and minimalistic interface do not meet the expectations of todayâ€™s digitally savvy customers. To address these challenges, the proposed solution is to create a modern, user-friendly website that effectively displays the salon's offerings and visual portfolio. This enhancement is expected to strengthen the salon's online presence, attract new clientele, and keep existing customers engaged with regularly updated content.
